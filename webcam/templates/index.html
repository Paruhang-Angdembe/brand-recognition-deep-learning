Beyond Logos Through Computer Vision

Objective

The goal is to create a computer vision system for real-time analysis of online brand mentions and consumer interactions, providing businesses with immediate insights into brand perception and consumer behavior. This solution aims to surpass traditional analysis methods by extracting actionable data from digital media, enabling strategic decision-making. With a focus on a user-friendly dashboard for data visualization, our project is designed to revolutionize brand analysis, driving market success and profitability in the digital era.
